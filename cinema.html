Cinema St. Agustí is located in the heart of the city, with a focus on independent and international cinema.
All movies are shown in their original language and in the open air, with a view of Tibidabo and the Sagrada Familia: firmly grounded in Barcelona, with a vision of the world.
+ PERSONAL CANON
Is it possible to define a general cinematic canon? At Cinema St. Agustí, we believe that the only canon which means anything at all is the personal one. This series is a monthly event where we invite a CSA employee to present a movie that has shaped their understanding of cinema (and life!).
¡Stop discussing what should be important to everyone and start discussing what's important to you!

Movies: Before Sunrise, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, and many more.
+ PELICULAS EN CASTELLANO
The staff of cinema St. Agustí is dedicated to practice their Spanish by watching movies. Some might say this is not the most efficient way to learn a new language, to which we respond "¡Claro que sí!"

Movies: Todo Sobre Mi Madre, Amores Perros, El espíritu de la colmena, and many more.
+ WIM WENDERS
The master of the road movie, the poet of the American West, the chronicler of the human soul, friend of Sibylle Baier. This series is dedicated to the work and life of Wim Wenders.
Every movie is accompanied by a short meditation on the importance of the color red, and everyone is given a complimentary red hat.

Movies: Paris; Texas, Alice in the Cities, Perfect Days, and many more.
+ PEOPLE-WATCHING
Watching cinema on a rooftop, have you dared to look past the screen at your surroundings? You might feel like you're in the Hitchcock classic Rear Window: observing the neighborhood through lighted-up windows in the night. We explore the voyeuristic side of cinema and its keen eye for human behavior. Who knows what you might see through our binoculars?

Movies: Rear Window, Nightcrawler, The Truman Show, and many more.
